About Scribble & Spark Co

Welcome to Scribble & Spark Co, where the ancient art of candle making meets modern design sensibilities. We are a boutique artisan company dedicated to creating exceptional handcrafted candles that illuminate not just your space, but your soul.

Founded by passionate artisans who believe in the power of handmade goods, we specialize in creating unique, high-quality candles using premium wax blends, carefully selected fragrances, and time-honored techniques that make each piece a work of art.

Every candle we create is a testament to our commitment to sustainability, quality, and the timeless beauty of handcrafted goods. We source our materials responsibly and pour our hearts into every creation.
