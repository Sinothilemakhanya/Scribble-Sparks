Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your candles burn?
Our candles have varying burn times depending on size. Small candles (4oz) burn for approximately 25-30 hours, medium candles (8oz) for 50-60 hours, and large candles (12oz) for 75-85 hours.
What materials do you use in your candles?
We use 100% natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and premium fragrance oils. All our materials are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and sourced from sustainable suppliers.
Do you offer custom scents?
Yes! We love creating custom scent blends for special occasions, events, or personal preferences. Custom orders require a minimum quantity and 2-3 weeks lead time.
